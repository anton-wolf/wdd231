Meal Schedule Site Plan

Purpose

To create an immersive and user-friendly online platform that stores your favourite dishes, ingredients, and serving count, additionally it allows you to monitor your cooking habits, ultimately influencing how you grocer as well.

Goals

  • Makes you aware of your dietary habits
  • Helps optimize your meal times by promoting a routine
  • Helps you control your food budget by ensuring you never over / under provision your supper meals
  • Improve family interaction by helping provide diverse, consistent nighttime meals.
  • Improves overall appetite by reducing stail dishes.

Target Audience

  • Demographics

    This platform caters to individuals and families who actively plan their meals, including:

    • Adults aged 18–45 who manage household meals and grocery shopping.
    • Health-conscious individuals looking to improve dietary habits.
    • Families with children who aim to maintain a balanced, diverse meal schedule.
    • People living alone who struggle with meal planning but want structured guidance.
    • Budget-conscious users aiming to reduce food waste and optimize spending.

  • Psychographics

    The audience consists of:

    • People who value efficiency and organization in their daily routines
    • Users who enjoy cooking and seek inspiration for meal ideas.
    • Individuals looking for a healthier lifestyle through mindful eating.
    • Those who struggle with decision fatigue and want structured meal planning.
    • Shoppers who wish to make strategic grocery purchases to avoid unnecessary expenses

  • User persons

    • Busy Professionals: Working individuals who want to streamline meal prep without sacrificing quality.
    • Health Enthusiasts: Users focused on maintaining balanced diets and tracking ingredient choices.
    • Families: Parents looking to improve meal consistency and create engaging dinner experiences.
    • Budget-Smart Consumers: Users wanting to optimize food spending and minimize waste

Scenarios

TThe website should help users answer key questions, such as:

  • "How can I quickly plan meals for the week?" Users should be able to store favorite meals, track ingredient availability, and generate structured meal plans effortlessly
  • "Am I repeating the same meals too often?" Users should get insights into their meal history and recommendations for fresh, diverse dish selections.
  • "How do I improve my family's mealtime experience?" Families can access meal rotation plans that encourage variety and interaction, ensuring a well-balanced approach to dinners.
